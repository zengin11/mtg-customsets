# Target format: Commander
- Single commanders, with a web of partner pairings

# Themes
- **Core theme**: Artifacts matter

# Custom mechanic

**Partner—X, Y**

Parnter—X, Y, Z... is the same as a creature having Partner—X, Partner—Y, and Partner—Z. So it can partner with any creature that shares one of those partner batches.
