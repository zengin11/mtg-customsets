-Nobara, Unswayed-
If I have Nobara out and lightning bolt your creature for 3 damage, you ALSO take 3 damage.

If I sweep five of your creatures with 2 damage apiece, you ALSO take 2 damage.

If I attack you with my 6/6, and you block with your 4/4, my guy deals 6 damage to your creature—so you ALSO take 6 damage—and yours deals 4 damage to mine—so I ALSO take 4 damage

A pretty wild effect. I got my ass beat the first time I ran her. Her effect is symmetrical, so it can help your opponents just as much. The goal is that you build around it better than they do with lots of targeted damage (for their stuff) and damage prevention (for your stuff). Red and White, respectively, are good at those though.

-Mai, Secret Shot-
Mai is cute. You get 2 mana for free by attacking, then can tap her to net one more if you're okay not having her as a blocker.
By doing both, you can barely add enough mana for her to deal a single damage to something. Which IMO is a pretty neat way of representing her one-time extra bullet. You're probably better spending the mana on other things, though. Pretty lore accurate I suppose.

-Noritoshi Kamo, Clan Leader-
USUALLY Persist is a one time deal. A creature dies, comes back a little weaker, then when it dies again it's gone. But if you can trigger Noritoshi's Renown, the +1/+1 and -1/-1 counters actually delete each other. Meaning that if Noritoshi dies again, he'll come back.

Funky kinda way to show his blood manipulation, tied in with him being head of his clan (thus making "renowned" kinda fitting)

I'm really proud of this design. You can trigger the protection with buff spells if you want; like hitting him with an Act of Heroism will give him protection from white for the future.

When he's fully protected from every color (and colorless), he's essentially got shroud, unblockable, and can't be dealt damage.
