Ninjago (NGO) is designed to play in a 5 color deck

# Colors: 5 color (WUBRG)

# Commander(s):
The Golden Ninja, Destined Hero // Lloyd, Ultimate Spinjitsu Master
- Use this commander if you want to focus on activated abilities

Partner—Sons of the First: Sensei Wu, Ancient Tutor and Lord Garmadon, Dark King
- Use these commanders if you want to focus on transforming.

# Themes
- Unblocked attackers
- Bounce (return to hand)
- Transforming
- Activated abilities

Spinjutsu combines these themes by returning an unblocked attacker to your hand, and transforming, as a single activated ability.

# Custom mechanic: Spinjutsu
Spinjutsu is very close to the real Ninjutsu mechanic, with one key exception: When playing a card from your hand with its Spinjutsu ability, it enters transformed. You can also play it normally and activate the ability on the battlefield to transform it later

# Cycles / card groups
- Monocolored Spinjutsu Ninja
- Seven ninjas with Spinjutsu. *Five cover all five colors: Cole (black), Jay (blue), Kai (red), Lloyd (green), Zane (white)*
- With two additional: *Nya (blue), and Morro (black + green)*
- Monocolored Unblockable Serpentine: *Four snakes all have an enters-the-battlefield ability, and an activated ability to make them unblockable.*
